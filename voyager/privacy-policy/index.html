Voyager Privacy Policy

This page describes how the mobile application Voyager Rocket Malfunction ("The App") is handling user data.

The App can be installed through Apple App Store.

Privacy Policy for Apple App Store

The App does not generate or store any data related to the user.

The only information saved on the device is a local highscore. This highscore is overwritten whenever a higher score is achieved.
